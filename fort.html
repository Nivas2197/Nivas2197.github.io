RAIGAD

Raigad Fort was the capital of the Maratha Empire from 1674 to 1818. It is located on a hilltop in the Sahyadri Mountains and is considered to be one of the most important forts in Indian history. The fort is well-preserved and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

SHIVNERI

Shivneri Fort is an ancient military fortification located near Junnar in Pune district in Maharashtra, India. It is the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire. The fort is situated on a hilltop that overlooks the Junnar city. It is a triangular-shaped fort with seven well-defended gates.
