    Ganpati Pule Beach

    Ratnagiri

    ABOUT

    Regarded as one of the finest beaches along the Konkan coast, Ganpatipule beach is known for its sparkling blue waters. The beach derives its name from the ancient Ganpatipule temple situated in the vicinity. Cheerful waves, a long stretch of golden and silver sands, lined with coconut and cashew trees, and tranquil vibes from all around are what make this trip an unforgettable experience. Relax at the beach basking in the sun or enjoy sipping on coconut water. Unlike many touristy beaches, there are only basic amenities available at this beach. You can see some small shops selling snacks and coconut water. Paid changing room facility can be availed at nominal charges.

    Best Time To Visit

    Winter months lasting November to February are considered the best months to visit Ganpatipule beach. The weather remains pleasant throughout the day but nights are colder. The summer season starts knocking from March and lasts till May. The months of March and April are also not bad for a trip but avoid May, which is the hottest month.
