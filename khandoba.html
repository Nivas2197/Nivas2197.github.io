    KHANDOBA MANDIR

    JEJURI

    ABOUT

    Khandoba Mandir, situated on a hill in Jejuri, Maharashtra, is a revered temple dedicated to Lord Khandoba, a popular deity among the farming and warrior communities. Often considered a form of Lord Shiva, Khandoba is worshipped for strength, protection, and prosperity.

    The temple is known for its scenic hilltop location, accessible via a series of steps, and offers panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Devotees throw turmeric (haldi) as an offering, which turns the entire temple complex a vibrant yellow during major festivals.

    Jejuri is especially lively during festivals like Champa Shashti and Somvati Amavasya, when thousands of pilgrims flock here. The devotion, energy, and color make Khandoba Mandir a unique spiritual destination.
